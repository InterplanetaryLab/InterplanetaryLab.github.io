# Specifications
 - Model: Thermotron Model S1.2
 - Temperature Range: -70c to 180c
 - Internal Volume: 1.2 ft^3 
   - Capable of fitting 1U to 3U Cubesats
 - GN2 Purging
- Wire feedthroughs
  - Thermocouples
  - Power
  - Custom
