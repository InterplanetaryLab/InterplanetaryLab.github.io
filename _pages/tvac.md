# Specifications
 - Volume of ~1 m^3
 - Thermal Control with range of 80C to -1100C
 - Thermal phase roll out: Currently in Phase 1A
    - Phase 1A: Platen, Insulation, Heaters, Initial Shroud, Electric Feedthroughs, & Computer Setup
    - Phase 1B: GN2 Plate, RGA, LN2 Feedthroughs, LN2 Cooling w/Platen, & Nipple Adapters for LN2
    - Phase 2: Full Shroud 
 - Lowest pressure tested 3.00e-3 torr
    - Goal range 7100 torr to 1.00e-5 torr
 - 9 Total ports
