# Specifications
 - Volume of ~1 m^3
 - Thermal Control (in development for Fall 2021).
 - Minimum Average Tested Pressure of 3.2 *10^-3 Torr.
   - Lowest tested 3.08 *10^-3 Torr.
 - Interior Lighting Control
 - 9 Ports
